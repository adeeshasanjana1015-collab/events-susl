About Events.SUSL

Events.SUSL is the official event management platform for Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, developed by the Department of Data Science, Faculty of Computing.

Our QR-based system makes it easy for students to discover events, register online, and check in instantly — eliminating paper tickets and manual attendance tracking.
